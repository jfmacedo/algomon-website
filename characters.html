Main Characters

Meet the four elemental Algomons who protect the magical forest. Each one has unique powers, strengths, and personality.

Fire Element

Flamaris

A brave and impulsive Algomon with a burning spirit. Flamaris uses fire-based attacks and fights with courage.

Power: 90

Speed: 75

Defense: 60

Water Element

Piscyra

A sensitive and magical Algomon connected to water and intuition. Piscyra is calm, caring, and powerful in support abilities.

Magic: 95

Speed: 70

Defense: 65

Earth Element

Terracorn

A strong and protective Algomon with a powerful connection to the earth. Terracorn is loyal, resistant, and excellent in defense.

Power: 85

Defense: 95

Speed: 45

Air Element

Aurelibra

A graceful and balanced Algomon who controls the power of air. Aurelibra is fast, elegant, and strategic in battle.

Speed: 95

Magic: 80

Defense: 60
