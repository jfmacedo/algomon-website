About Algomon

Algomon is a magical universe built around elemental creatures known as Algomons. Each Algomon represents a force of nature and has its own personality, abilities, and story. The project combines fantasy, adventure, and interactivity to create a visual experience inspired by modern game websites.

The Story

In an enchanted forest hidden between worlds, four legendary Algomons reside: Flamaris, Terracorn, Aurelibra, and Piscyra. They protect the balance of the forest and represent the four essential elements of nature.

Flamaris embodies the power of fire, being brave and intense. Terracorn represents the earth, symbolizing strength, protection, and resilience. Aurelibra controls the air, bringing balance, speed, and elegance. Piscyra holds the power of water, characterized by intuition, sensitivity, and magical abilities.

Project Purpose

This website was initially created as part of a Client Side Programming academic project. The first version aimed to demonstrate knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, including page navigation, interactive elements, and visual design.

Currently, the project is being refined as part of my personal portfolio. The focus is now on transforming Algomon into a more complete, modern, and professional experience, with improved UI/UX, interactivity, responsiveness, and visual identity.

Future Vision

The evolution of Algomon includes new pages, animations, improved user experience, a more advanced character selection system, and potential playable elements in the future.
